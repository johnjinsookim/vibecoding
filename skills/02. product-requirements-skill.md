# Product Requirements Document

> Goal: Convert strategy into a clear, structured, interview-ready PRD.
> Keep it concise, concrete, and measurable.
> With inputs from Product Strategy doc.

---

# 1. Executive Summary

**What are we building?**  
One paragraph summary.

**Who is it for?**  
Primary user.

**What problem are we solving?**  
Core pain point.

**Why now?**  
Strategic reason.

**Expected impact:**  
North Star Metric, and why this metric was picked.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

## Target User
- Who are they?
- What are they trying to accomplish?

## Current Pain
- How do they solve this today?
- What’s broken?
- Why is it frustrating / costly?

---

# 3. Goals & Non-Goals

## Goals (Must Be Measurable)
- Goal 1 (e.g., Increase X from A → B)
- Goal 2
- Goal 3

## Non-Goals
- Explicitly out of scope
- What we are NOT solving

---

# 4. Success Metrics

## Primary Metric (North Star)
- What single metric best measures success?
- Define how it is calculated.

## Health Metrics
- Executive summary
- List in table

## Countermetrics
- Executive summary
- List in table

# 6. Solution Overview

## What Are We Building?
High-level description.

## Key Features (MVP)
- Feature 1: high-level overview, then list detailed user stories and acceptance criteria
- Feature 2: high-level overview, then list detailed user stories and acceptance criteria
- Feature 3: high-level overview, then list detailed user stories and acceptance criteria

Each feature should clearly tie to a goal.

## In Scope (MVP)
Bullet list.

## Out of Scope
Bullet list.

---

# 8. Risks & Tradeoffs

## Key Risks
- Adoption risk
- Technical risk
- Business risk

## Tradeoffs
- What alternatives did we consider?
- Why this approach?

---

# 9. Rollout Plan

- Experiment or full launch?
- How do we measure success?
- What happens if it fails?

---

# Interview Quality Checklist

Before finalizing, ensure:

- [ ] Clear user
- [ ] Clear problem
- [ ] Measurable goals
- [ ] Defined North Star
- [ ] Explicit scope
- [ ] Risks acknowledged
- [ ] No vague language

---

# Output Constraint

Keep the full PRD under 3 pages.
Be crisp.
Be concrete.
Avoid fluff.
